A fast, cozy tracker for Steam achievements — notes, tags, filters, friend compare, and more.

Download for Windows
Open-source on GitHub • No telemetry • Local notes/tags

What makes KSAT nice?

Notes & Tags

Keep per-game notes, tag cleanup or DLC goals, and search across them fast.

Smart Filters

Unlocked/locked/secret/manual-only, plus “next easiest” or “rarest first”.

Friend Compare

Paste a SteamID64 or vanity and see exactly who’s missing what.

Why people like KSAT

  • Manual overrides when APIs lag.
  • Launch & Store shortcuts per game.
  • Rarity & ordering (next-easiest / rarest-first).
  • Themeable (Neon, Midnight, Cozy, Solar + extras).
  • Share Card to show off your progress.
